The **Amateur Brewing Competition of the NT** has wrapped up for 2025, and we’d like to extend a huge thank you to everyone who took part.

This year’s competition featured **75 entries** judged across a broad range of beer, mead and a few ginger beer styles, with **37 registered participants, judges, and stewards** contributing to a smooth and successful event.

The overall quality of entries was excellent, showcasing not just the variety but also the creativity and skill of the Territory's homebrewing community.

---

## 🏆 Best of Show – Beer

**Stuart McLay**  
**_Salty Plum Sour_**  
*Straight Sour Beer (BJCP 28D)*

Entries with two bottles were eligible for the best of show, with the top scorers in each category and special judge nominations placed into a knock-out judging session by a panel of judges. Unlike other flights this was not scored according to category but a vote of judge preference across the panel. With the final selection earning Stuart's *Salty Plum Sour* the title of **Best of Show**.

---

## 🍺 Overall Brewer

**Jon Clark – Overall Brewer**

With multiple category wins and placings across the competition, **Jon Clark** was awarded **Overall Brewer** for 2025.
