The Darwin & NT Homebrewers Annual Case Swap was held on the 15th of September, and once again the club delivered an outstanding and diverse lineup of beers for particpants to enjoy over the Christmas break.

With classic European styles through to experimental and mixed-fermentation brews, participants took home a great spread of beers. The chat on facebook messenger is already filled with reviews and discssions as brewers enjoy their beers.

A huge thanks to everyone who brewed, shared, and helped make the day such a success.
